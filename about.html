"OUR STORY"

QUBE Kaffaine & Kitchen is an exciting new venture that brings restaurant-inspired service to a relaxing, cafe setting. Our menu is influenced by the flavours of the Mediterranean, infused & served with a modern zest.


Featuring an open plan kitchen, our experienced chefs create rich, home-made dishes every day from only the freshest of ingredients. Our pursuit of an uncompromised dining experience leads us to prepare our ingredients daily, leaving our guests only the fullest of flavours. We are also proud to be able to offer an open dietary experience with a wealth of gluten-free, vegetarian and halal options available for our guests.


We know no compromise on quality, that's why we have exclusively selected Toby's Estate coffee beans as our sole supplier for our guests.

Toby's Estate harvests the finest beans from their multi-award winning coffee plantation Finca Santa Teresa in Panama, and their pursuit for perfection and uncompromised quality blends perfectly with us at QUBE.


At the hands of our experienced barista, we're proud to offer a robust and flavourful coffee to complement your experience with us.
